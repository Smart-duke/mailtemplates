Careers in Data Science

The most common careers in data science include the following roles.

Data Scientist

Determine what the problem is, what questions need answers, and where to find the data.

ML Scientist

They develop autonomous AI software and conduct tests to ensure that software generates accurate predictions.

Data Analyst

Analysts bridge the gap between the data scientists and the business analysts, organizing and analyzing data to answer the questions the organization poses.

Data Architect

A data architect is “an individual who is responsible for designing, creating, deploying and managing an organization’s data architecture.

Data Engineer

Data engineers focus on developing, deploying, managing, and optimizing the organization’s data infrastructure and data pipelines.
